⚠️ Common Issues
"Windows protected your PC" warning?

Click "More info"
Click "Run anyway"
This is a false positive (very common with PyInstaller)

Build failed with errors?
Try this more detailed command:
pyinstaller --onefile --console --name "PasswordManager" --hidden-import=cryptography --hidden-import=cryptography.fernet --hidden-import=colorama --hidden-import=tabulate password_manager.py



.exe won't run?

Check Windows Defender didn't quarantine it
Try running as Administrator
